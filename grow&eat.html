Grow & Eat

Easy fruits and herbs you can grow right at home, from windowsill to table.

Strawberries

  • Light: Full sun, 6 to 8 hours
  • Water: Every 2 to 3 days
  • Care: Plant in well-drained soil, remove dead leaves regularly.
Ready to harvest: 4 to 6 weeks after flowering

Blueberries

  • Light: Full sun
  • Water: 1 to 2 inches per week
  • Care: Needs acidic soil, plant two varieties for better yield.
Ready to harvest: 60 to 90 days after flowering

Mint

  • Light: Partial to full sun
  • Water: Every 2 to 3 days
  • Care: Grows fast, keep in a separate pot to prevent spreading.
Ready to harvest: 90 days from planting

Cilantro

  • Light: Full sun to partial shade
  • Water: Every 2 to 3 days
  • Care: Prefers cool weather, harvest leaves before it flowers.
Ready to harvest: 45 to 70 days from planting
