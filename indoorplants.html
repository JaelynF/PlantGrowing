Plants for Every Corner

No matter the size of your space or how much light you get, there is a plant that will thrive right where you are. These beginner friendly picks are low maintenance, forgiving, and perfect for anyone just starting out.

Pothos

  • Light: Low to bright indirect
  • Water: Every 1 to 2 weeks
  • Care: Let soil dry out between waterings. Wipe leaves monthly.

Snake Plant

  • Light: Low to bright indirect
  • Water: Every 2 to 3 weeks
  • Care: Tolerates neglect, avoid overwatering, great for bedrooms.

Monstera

  • Light: Bright indirect
  • Water: Once a week
  • Care: Loves humidity, wipe leaves, give it space to grow.

Peace Lily

  • Light: Low to medium indirect
  • Water: Once a week
  • Care: Droops when thirsty, a natural reminder to water. Keep away from pets.
