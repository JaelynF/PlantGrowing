Quick Tips

Fast facts, no fluff.

Why are my plant's leaves turning yellow?

Yellow leaves usually mean you are overwatering. Let the soil dry out completely between waterings and make sure your pot has drainage holes.

My pot has no drainage hole what do I do?

Add a layer of pebbles at the bottom of the pot to create a small reservoir and prevent roots from sitting in standing water.

Why do I need to wipe my plant's leaves?

Dusty leaves block light and reduce the plant's ability to photosynthesize. Wipe them with a damp cloth once a month to keep them clean and healthy.

I just moved my plant and it looks sad is it dying?

Probably not! Plants go through a period of adjustment when moved to a new spot. Give it about 2 weeks to settle before worrying.

Does talking to plants actually help?

Yes! The CO₂ you exhale and the vibrations from your voice can actually encourage plant growth. So keep talking to them.

Why do my plant's tips turn brown?

Brown tips usually mean the air around your plant is too dry. Try placing a humidifier nearby or setting the pot on a tray of pebbles with water.

Can I use regular potting mix for succulents?

No succulents need gritty, fast-draining soil. Regular potting mix holds too much moisture and can cause root rot. Use a cactus or succulent mix instead.

How do I make sure my plant gets even light?

Rotate your plant a quarter turn every week so all sides get equal exposure to light. This prevents lopsided growth and keeps the plant full and balanced.
